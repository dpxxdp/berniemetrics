Here at Berniemetrics we believe in open access to information. Our numbers are your numbers: do what you can with them and share your results with the community!

In order for everyone to get along, we need to lay a few ground rules. We ask that you take care not to hit our server more than once per 2 seconds in any continuously running process. We will actively block anyone who abuses these rules, but we'd like to spend our resources bringing the bern instead of patrolling the community.
