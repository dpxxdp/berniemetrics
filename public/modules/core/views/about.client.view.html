About Berniemetrics


We are a group of volunteers excited to compile and visualize the numbers surrounding the Bernie Sanders run for the presidency.

If you have any experience with graphic design or web design we could especially use your help! Please send us an email at berniemetrics@gmail.com or sign up and leave us a message.

The content on berniemetrics.org is not a reflection of Senator Sanders, his office, or his campaign. This website is made and maintained by volunteers with no relation to or association with Bernie Sanders.
